Bowling Scorekeeper

Link to website

I love bowling!

As of 1/19/23, I have just hit a personal best score of 248! It feels like I've been improving rapidly, but how can I know for sure? I created a bowling app to score and save games, so you can review previous games and see stats. I have not yet implemented many useful statistics, but I plan on having comparisons between time periods for stats such as average, strike count and amount of spare opportunities missed. I have to do more research on bowling analysis.

How it works

This is a simple Vite React application. I decided to try typescript and found it useful for debugging. It's hosted on vercel, as well as the api that it uses to connect to MongoDB. Vercel is not the fastest when making api calls, but it's free. The app took about a day and a half to write and could definitely be neatened up, but it works for my needs. I plan to keep working on this app and eventually present it to the bowling club at WPI.

45 Days, 45 Songs

Check out one of our albums!

Emergency Stop

Last Autumn, 2022, my roommate and I achieved my proudest musical accomplishment. For 45 days, we wrote and recorded a song. Over the past few months, I've produced, mixed, and refined parts when necessary. However, 99% of what you hear was recorded on the day.

Producing these songs has been a great lesson on song writing, collaboration, and production.

We had enough material to release two albums on spotify under the band name "Emergency Stop". The explanation behind the name is that we were both taking a machining class and were forced to press the emergency stop button many times.
