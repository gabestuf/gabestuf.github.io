The LED Board

When I first arrived at Worcester Polytechnic Institute in 2019, I knew next to nothing about Computer Science. This project's main contributors were Jake, Zach, and Ted, who wired the board, programmed the raspberry pi, and designed the entire system. The system featured a web app which could change the text, which could scroll across the board or do a number of other animations. Between each LED are diffusers made of paper and tape. That's the part I helped with. Though my contribution was small, it was fascinating to be part of this team.
